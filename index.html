About Me

I offer text digitization services using advanced OCR and HWR technologies, specializing in extracting information from handwritten records and PDFs. My expertise includes developing Python-based tools and web applications for data extraction. My primary focus is supporting heritage and community projects by providing seamless digitization solutions.

Whether it's fieldwork notes or historic grave record documents, I ensure accurate text extraction and data compilation into user-friendly formats such as CSV. With experience in frontend and backend development, I create web applications with intuitive interfaces, allowing for uploads directly from the field and real-time data processing.

Clients benefit from reliable support, including advanced debugging and problem-solving, backed by my experience with large-scale enterprise applications. I strive to provide top-notch services, focusing on data accuracy, security, and customer satisfaction.

If you'd like to learn more about my services or discuss a project, email me at me@danieltierney.ie. I'm happy to answer any questions and explore how I can help with your text digitization needs.
